The Grand Midori Rising in Ortigas

Federal Land, Inc. brings its first foray in the heart of the Ortigas Central Business District, featuring authentic Japanese modern architecture and design.

Renowned Japanese architectural firm Tange Associates was tapped as design consultant to ensure world-class aesthetics and innovation. Interiors and landscape draw inspiration from the Japanese concepts of 'wabi-sabi' (beauty in imperfection) and 'miyabi' (expression of elegant refinement), creating a truly harmonious living experience.
